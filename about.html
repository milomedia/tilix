A Team with Focus & Intensity

The firm was founded by Neil Williams and comprises a growing roster of associates and support staff.

The Tilix team is directed and controlled by:

  • Neil Williams: has formidable experience in enterprise architecture, electrical engineering and innovation management.
  • Duncan Tytler: is an authority in the energy services market and demand side management.
  • Andrew Hancock: is an expert in leading front and back office operations around the supply of gas and electricity.

The Tilix team has experience in energy supply, demand side management and clean-tech.

Our solution expertise focuses around innovation and technology in both the demand and supply sides of gas and electricity.

Day to day, Tilix works with the ideas, innovation and technology that will solve global energy problems and create opportunities to make life better for everyone.

Reinventing Fire and Making Fossil Fuel Obsolete

Technologies such as renewables, waste to gas, electric vehicles combined with smart, digital control of energy is giving us clean air, energy security and create enormous wealth. In the long run, this is a key component of abating climate change and moving to a more sustainable planet.

Tilix operates on the basis that we are in a race against time rather than in competition with other firms.

Please reach out to us if you share our passion, sense of urgency, constancy of purpose and desire to work in partnership.
