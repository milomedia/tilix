Sponsored Research

The whole energy system is going through major transformations and the rate of change is increasing exponentially.

Therefore, research has never been more critical in helping government and industry increase the stock of knowledge on local, regional, national and international problems and opportunities in energy.

Key Challenges

Whilst the impact of technology has been profound, it is clear that we are just getting started on the journey to creating an energy system that is clean, cheap and cheerful.

In recent years, by far the most common challenge for movers and shakers in the energy industry has been keeping up with the pace of change.

Outcomes

By investing in Tilix Sponsored Research, our clients access the knowledge, expertise and insight they need to address the commercial and technological challenges facing their smart energy or cleantech businesses.

The value and impact Tilix delivers to its Sponsored Research clients includes:

  • a strong evidence base to support business growth.
  • high quality reporting about technical and commercial challenges.
  • stronger strategic and tactical planning.
  • viewpoints spanning 3 horizons: short, medium and long term.
  • new opportunities to capture the benefits of sustaining and disruptive innovation.
  • engage and collaborate at the cutting edge with other stakeholders from business, government, academia and not for profits.
